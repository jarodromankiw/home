Chicken Gyros

Blah blah blah

Ingredients

    Chicken

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp dried rosemary
  • Kosher salt & fresh ground pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 chicken breasts

    • Gyro (Naan Bread)

  • 1/4 ounce packet regular yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water, about 110 degrees
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2-1/4 cups (338g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup non-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

    For the Chicken

  1. Measure all ingredients (except chicken) in a measuring cup, and stir until well mixed.
  2. Pound chicken breasts to an even thickness, and place in a ziplock bag. Fill the bag with the marinade. Rest in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  3. 30 minutes before ready to cook, remove the chicken from the fridge to lower the temperature.
  4. Heat grill on high, and cook until chicken is done. Use the remaining marinade in the ziplock to brush on the chicken before removing from the grill.
  5. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before chopping into 1/2 inch cubes.

    6. For the Gyros

  6. Combine yeast, water, and sugar and mix with a fork until fully integrated. Set aside for 10 minutes or until foamy.
  7. In a stand mixer, add flour, yogurt, oil, salt, and yeast mixture. Mix with the dough hook for 10 minutes on the lowest setting. Remove the dough from the bowl and form a ball. Lightly oil a bowl and place the dough in the bowl with plastic wrap on top. Place in a warm place (Turn the oven on for 30 seconds and then turn it off) for 60 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  8. Place on a floured surface and cut it into 8 pieces. Form balls with the small pieces of dough.
  9. Form thin discs (about 6 inches in diameter) and cook on a medium-hot, oiled pan until the dough becomes bubbly. Flip and cook the other side and serve.
