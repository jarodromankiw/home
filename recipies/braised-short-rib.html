Short Ribs

A tender and savory dish featuring boneless short ribs braised with red wine and vegetables, perfect for a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large boneless short ribs
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • ½ yellow onion, chopped
  • ½ container (3 oz) tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 375 ml Cabernet Sauvignon red wine
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 1 sprig oregano
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Season the short ribs with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a large oven-safe pot over medium-high heat. Brown the short ribs on all sides, then set them aside.
  4. Add the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic to the pot. Sauté for about 5 minutes, until softened.
  5. Stir in the tomato paste and flour, cooking for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Pour in the red wine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.
  7. Add the beef broth, oregano, and thyme. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  8. Return the short ribs to the pot, cover, and transfer to the oven.
  9. Braise for 2.5-3 hours, until the short ribs are tender and the sauce is thickened.
  10. Remove the short ribs from the pot, skim off any excess fat from the sauce, and serve with the sauce poured over the ribs.
