Tomato Basil Pasta with Mozzarella

This tomato basil pasta with mozzarella is a delightful and creamy dish, perfect for a cozy dinner. The combination of fresh tomatoes, fragrant basil, and creamy mozzarella makes it an instant favorite.

Ingredients

    Pasta

  • 2 lb pasta (cook 2 minutes shorter than box instructions)
  • 8 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1.5 teaspoons oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1.5 cups white wine
  • 10 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 6 cups heavy cream
  • 1.5 cups Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
  • Fresh mozzarella, for garnish

Instructions

    For the Pasta

  1. Cook the pasta according to the box instructions, but reduce the cooking time by 2 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat a generous amount of extra virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the halved cherry tomatoes, salt, red pepper flakes, oregano, and black pepper. Cook until the tomatoes begin to soften, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the white wine to the pan and let it simmer for about 3-4 minutes, allowing the alcohol to evaporate and the sauce to reduce slightly.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, mixing it thoroughly with the tomatoes and wine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously to combine. Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  6. Add the shredded Parmesan cheese to the sauce and stir until itâ€™s melted and well combined.
  7. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce, ensuring that it is well coated. Cook together for 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  8. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and slices of fresh mozzarella.
