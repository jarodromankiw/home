Homemade Pasta Dough (Serves 4)

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 400g all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. On a clean surface, make a mound with 400g of flour and create a well in the center.
  2. Crack 4 eggs into the well.
  3. Using a fork, gently whisk the eggs, gradually incorporating the flour from the edges until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until itâ€™s smooth and elastic.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  6. Once rested, roll out the dough and cut it into your desired pasta shape.

Recommended Thinness Settings for Pasta:

  • For lasagna: 2nd thinnest setting on your pasta machine.
  • For linguini: 2nd thinnest setting on your pasta machine.
  • For spaghetti: 4th thinnest setting on your pasta machine.
