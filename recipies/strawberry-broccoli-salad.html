Strawberry Broccoli Salad

Broccoli Salad with Creamy Poppy Seed Dressing will be one of the best salads you ever make – guaranteed! It’s the perfect easy, make ahead, texture packed side dish or potluck favorite! It’s packed with fresh strawberries, creamy avocado, crunchy pears, apples, salty sunflower seeds, sweet dried cranberries and tangy feta!

Ingredients

    Salad

  • 5 cups chopped broccoli florets (blanched)
  • 1-pound chopped strawberries
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 pear, chopped
  • 1 sweet apple, chopped
  • 1 large Avocado
  • 3/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta

    • Dressing

  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

Instructions

    For the Salad

  1. Add all the salad ingredients to a large bowl except avocados.
  2. In a separate medium bow, whisk together the dressing ingredients. Add desired amount of dressing to salad and toss until evenly coated. Best if chilled for at least 30 minutes up to overnight. Toss with avocados just before serving.
