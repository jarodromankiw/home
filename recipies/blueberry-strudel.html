Blueberry Strudel

Ingredients

Strudel

  • 90g flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 5 tbsp butter (70g), melted
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup sugar (100g)

Topping (Crumble)

  • 35g flour
  • 35g dark brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar (18g)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp butter (42g)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a baking dish or line with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix the melted butter, vanilla, and sugar until smooth. Add the egg and mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and stir until a smooth batter forms.
  5. Gently fold in the frozen blueberries.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish.

    7. For the Crumble Topping

  7. In a separate bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.
  8. Add the butter and mix until the mixture forms a crumbly texture.
  9. Sprinkle the crumble evenly over the batter.
  10. Bake the strudel for 35-40 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean.
  11. Let it cool slightly before serving.
