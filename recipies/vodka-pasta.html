Vodka Pasta

Serves 2-3 people

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb pasta
  • 1/8 onion (25g)
  • 2 tsp (12g) garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp (13g) extra virgin olive oil (EVOO)
  • 1/4 cup (65g) tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp (35g) flour
  • 1/3 cup (80g) vodka
  • 1 1/4 cups (300g) milk
  • 1/4 cup (30g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 tsp (1.5g) red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cook pasta according to package instructions, stopping 2 minutes short of al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water and set the pasta aside.
  2. Step 2: Heat EVOO in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced onion and garlic, cooking until softened and fragrant.
  3. Step 3: Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing it to caramelize slightly.
  4. Step 4: Sprinkle in the flour and mix well to create a roux. Cook for 1 minute to remove the raw flour taste.
  5. Step 5: Pour in the vodka and stir, allowing it to reduce by half.
  6. Step 6: Gradually whisk in the milk to create a smooth sauce. Let it simmer until slightly thickened.
  7. Step 7: Stir in Parmesan cheese and red pepper flakes. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  8. Step 8: Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce, adding reserved pasta water as needed to achieve the desired consistency. Cook for 1-2 minutes to coat the pasta evenly.
  9. Step 9: Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
