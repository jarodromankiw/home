Soft Pretzels

Ingredients

    Pretzel Buns

  • 3/4 cup + 1 tablespoon (200g) water
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2-1/4 cup (310g) all-purpose flour
  • 1-1/8 tsp (1/2 packet) instant yeast
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Water Bath

  • 2 quarts (1900 ml) water
  • 1/4 cup (57g) baking soda

Topping

  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • Coarse salt

Instructions

  1. In a mixing cup, add warm water (about 110˚F) and stir in brown sugar. Once dissolved, add yeast and stir gently. Let the mixture rest for 10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a stand mixer, add flour and salt, then add the water-yeast mixture. Mix or knead for 10 minutes until a dough ball forms.
  3. Let the dough rise in a lightly greased bowl for 1 hour, or until doubled.
  4. Deflate the dough by punching the center.
  5. Divide into 5 pieces and shape each into a smooth ball or log.
  6. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet, cover, and let rest for 15 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 400˚F.
  8. Boil water and baking soda in a pot.
  9. Drop buns into boiling water one at a time. Cook for 30 seconds per side, then remove with a slotted spoon and place back on the baking sheet.
  10. Brush buns with melted butter, sprinkle with coarse salt, and score the tops with a knife.
  11. Bake for 20-24 minutes or until deep brown. Transfer to a cooling rack.
