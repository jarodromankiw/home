Double Chocholate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

    Cookies

  • 113g unsalted butter, fridge-cold and cubed
  • 80g soft light brown sugar
  • 80g caster sugar
  • 200g dark chocolate chips
  • 165g plain flour
  • 35g cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg

Instructions

    For the Pretzels

  1. In a mixing cup, add warm water (about 110˚F) and stir in brown sugar. Once dissolved, add yeast and stir in gently. Let the yeast, sugar, water mixture rest for 10 minutes, or until there is significant foaming on the top of the water
  2. In a stand mixer add flour and salt. Mix the flour and salt to ensure even distribution. Then add the water, yeast, and sugar mixture and mix until doug ball forms. Allow the dough to rise in a lightly greased bowl for 1-hour, or until doubled.
  3. Deflate the dough by punching the center of the dough ball. Divide the dough into 8 pieces, and shape each piece into desired shape. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and place the balls on the baking sheet, cover, and let rest for 15 minutes. Preheat oven to 400˚F
  4. Measure ingredients in water ball, and bring to a boil.
  5. Drop balls in 1 at a time and cook for 30 seconds, flip over, and cook for another 30 seconds. Using a meshed spoon, return the buns to the baking sheet
  6. Spread whisked egg on top of each bun followed by a generous sprinkle of coarse salt. Score the tops of the buns with a sharp knife.
  7. Bake the buns for 20 - 24 minutes, or until they are a deep brown. Remove from the oven and transfer to a cooling rack.
