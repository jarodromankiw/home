Chicken Marsala with Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thin
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (EVOO)
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/2 lb pasta (cooked 2 minutes short of al dente)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup half-and-half
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, but stop cooking 2 minutes short of al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp EVOO in a large pan over medium heat. Sear the thin-sliced chicken breasts until golden brown, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add Marsala wine and chicken broth. Simmer for 5 minutes to reduce slightly.
  4. Stir in butter and half-and-half, and let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes to thicken.
  5. Mix cornstarch with reserved pasta water, ensuring it's well dissolved.
  6. Add the cornstarch mixture into the pan and stir continuously to thicken the sauce further.
  7. Return the chicken to the pan and coat it in the sauce. Let everything cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Mix the cooked pasta into the sauce and serve hot.
